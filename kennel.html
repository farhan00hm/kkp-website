Welcome to Our Kennel

At KKP Kennel, we are dedicated to raising healthy, well-socialized dogs with excellent temperaments and sound health. Our focus is on quality breeding of German Shepherds and Labrador Retrievers that excel as both working dogs and family companions.

Our dogs live in a natural farm environment where they have plenty of space to exercise, play, and express their natural behaviors. Each of our dogs has a distinct personality and role at the farm, from guardians protecting our livestock to ambassadors welcoming visitors.

We follow responsible breeding practices, with careful selection of breeding pairs based on temperament, health, and conformation. Each puppy born at KKP receives excellent care, proper socialization, and complete health checks before going to their forever homes.

Adoption Process

We want to ensure that our puppies go to loving, prepared homes where they will thrive. Our adoption process is designed to match each puppy with the right family.

1

Inquiry

Start by contacting us about available puppies or upcoming litters. We'll ask about your living situation, experience with dogs, and what you're looking for in a companion.

2

Reservation

Once we've determined a good match, you can reserve a puppy with a deposit. We'll keep you updated with photos and progress reports as your puppy grows.

3

Going Home

Puppies go to their new homes at 8-10 weeks of age. They come with vaccination records, health certificate, deworming treatment, microchip, and a starter pack with food and care instructions.

What's Included
  • Health Certificate

    Veterinary examination and health certificate issued shortly before going home

  • Vaccinations & Deworming

    Age-appropriate vaccinations and multiple deworming treatments

  • Microchip

    Permanent identification for safety and security

  • Health Guarantee

    30-day health guarantee against congenital issues

  • Starter Pack

    Food sample, care instructions, and training tips

  • Lifetime Support

    Ongoing advice and assistance throughout your dog's life
