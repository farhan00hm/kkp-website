Our Story

KKP – Khundkar Khamar Prakalpa began as a vision to create an agricultural farm that prioritizes ethical animal treatment and sustainable farming practices. Located in Chandina, Cumilla, our farm has grown from a small homestead operation to a diverse agro initiative serving both local communities and urban centers.

We believe in producing high-quality, healthy products through responsible farming methods that respect the natural environment and animal welfare. Our commitment to free-range practices and organic feeding ensures that our products are not only nutritious but also ethically produced.

Today, KKP operates across multiple sectors including dairy, poultry, cattle, and kennel services, while maintaining our founding principles of sustainability and ethical farming.

Our Mission

To provide fresh, organic, and ethically produced farm products while promoting sustainable agricultural practices in Bangladesh.

Sustainable Farming

We implement eco-friendly farming practices that minimize environmental impact while maximizing productivity and natural resources.

Animal Welfare

Our animals enjoy natural, stress-free environments with free-ranging practices that allow them to express their natural behaviors.

Quality Products

We take pride in delivering premium quality, nutritious farm products from our farm directly to your table.

Our Farm Operations

Dairy Operations

Our dairy operations focus on producing fresh milk, yogurt, and ghee from free-range cattle. We are transitioning from traditional hand-milking to machine-milking to enhance efficiency while maintaining product quality. Our cattle are fed with locally sourced, natural feed to ensure the highest quality dairy products.

Poultry Operations

Our poultry section includes chickens, ducks, and geese that are raised in free-range environments. This approach not only ensures better animal welfare but also results in superior quality eggs and meat. Our birds are fed with natural grains and have access to open areas where they can forage naturally.

Cattle Raising

We raise cattle for both dairy production and meat sales, including specialized Qurbani cattle. Our animals are raised using ethical practices, with proper veterinary care and adequate space to graze and move freely. This approach ensures healthier animals and better quality meat.

Kennel Services

Our kennel specializes in breeding and raising healthy puppies of select breeds, including German Shepherds and Labrador Retrievers. All our puppies receive proper vaccinations, deworming treatments, and socialization to ensure they grow into well-adjusted dogs. We provide complete documentation and post-adoption support.

Additional Operations

Beyond our core operations, KKP is also involved in fisheries and agricultural crop production. While these products are primarily for internal use at present, we have plans to expand these operations and include them in our commercial offerings in the future. We are committed to applying the same sustainable and ethical practices across all our farming activities.

Looking to the Future

At KKP, we are continuously evolving and expanding our operations to better serve our customers while maintaining our commitment to sustainable farming. Our future plans include:

  • Expanding our dairy processing capabilities to offer more variety of products
  • Increasing our poultry operations to meet growing demand for free-range eggs and meat
  • Developing our fisheries and agricultural crop sections for commercial offerings
  • Implementing more advanced technology for improved farm management and product quality
  • Creating educational programs to promote sustainable farming practices in the community

We remain dedicated to our core values while embracing innovation to create a more sustainable and ethical agricultural model for Bangladesh.
