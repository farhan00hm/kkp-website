About KKP

KKP is an integrated agro farm located in Keronkhal, Chandina, Cumilla, promoting organic feeding and ethical treatment of animals through free-ranging practices. We specialize in dairy, poultry, and kennel operations.

Our Values

  • Local Feed: We use locally sourced, natural feed for our animals
  • Free Range: Our animals are allowed to roam freely in natural environments
  • Ethical Practices: We maintain the highest standards of animal welfare
  • Quality Products: We produce premium quality, healthy products
