About KKP

KKP - Khundkar Khamar Prakalpa is an agro farm promoting organic feeding and ethical treatment of animals through free-ranging practices.

Our farm specializes in dairy production (milk, yogurt, ghee), poultry operations (eggs, chicken, duck, goose), and a professional kennel. We also raise cattle for meat and Qurbani sales.

At KKP, we believe in sustainable farming practices, using local feed, and ensuring our animals enjoy a natural, stress-free environment.
