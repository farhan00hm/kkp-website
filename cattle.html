Our Cattle Farming Practices

Free-Range Practices

Our cattle are free to roam and graze in spacious pastures. This traditional farming method allows our animals to express their natural behaviors, resulting in healthier cattle and better quality products. We believe that happy cows produce better milk and meat.

Organic Feeding

We feed our cattle with locally sourced, organic feed free from synthetic chemicals and growth hormones. Our feed includes fresh grass, natural hay, and organic grains that provide complete nutrition. This natural diet ensures healthier animals and more nutritious products.

Comprehensive Healthcare

Regular veterinary check-ups and preventive care are essential parts of our cattle management. We maintain detailed health records for each animal and follow strict protocols to prevent disease. When medical treatment is necessary, we use it responsibly to ensure animal welfare.

Sustainable Practices

Our cattle farming incorporates sustainable practices that minimize environmental impact. We implement rotational grazing, water conservation, and waste management systems. Our goal is to maintain a healthy balance between productive farming and environmental stewardship.

Our Dairy Production

Fresh, Organic Dairy Products

Our dairy cattle produce some of the freshest and most nutritious milk available. We are transitioning from traditional hand-milking to modern machine-milking to enhance efficiency while maintaining the highest quality standards.

Our Dairy Process:

  1. Our cattle graze on organic pastures free from pesticides and chemicals
  2. Milk is collected twice daily under hygienic conditions
  3. Immediate cooling and processing to maintain freshness
  4. Minimal processing to preserve natural nutrients and flavors
  5. Products are packaged and delivered promptly to ensure freshness

From our dairy production, we offer fresh milk, yogurt, and traditional ghee. All our dairy products are minimally processed to maintain their natural goodness and nutritional value.
